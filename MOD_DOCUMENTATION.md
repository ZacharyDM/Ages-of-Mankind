# Ages of Mankind - 1 AD Bookmark Documentation

## Overview
This Crusader Kings 3 mod implements a comprehensive historical framework for playing in the year 1 AD, during the height of the Roman Empire and the dawn of the common era. The mod focuses on historical accuracy while maintaining engaging gameplay.

## Features Implemented

### 1 AD Bookmark
- **5 Playable Rulers** representing major civilizations of the era:
  - **Augustus Caesar** (Roman Empire) - Easy difficulty
  - **Arminius** (Cherusci Germanic tribe) - Moderate difficulty
  - **Vonones I** (Parthian Empire) - Hard difficulty
  - **Amanirenas** (Kingdom of Kush) - Moderate difficulty
  - **Emperor Ping** (Han China) - Easy difficulty

### Historical Cultures
**Mediterranean Cultures:**
- Roman - The dominant imperial culture with advanced civic traditions
- Greek - Philosophical culture maintaining intellectual traditions under Roman rule
- Gallic - Celtic warriors of Gaul, recently conquered by Rome
- Carthaginian - Merchant culture with maritime traditions

**Northern Cultures:**
- Cherusci - Germanic tribe famous for resisting Roman expansion
- Suebi - Large Germanic confederation known for warrior culture
- Briton - Celtic peoples of Britain maintaining druidic traditions
- Pictish - Northern British tribes with unique matrilineal customs

**Eastern Cultures:**
- Parthian - Persian empire controlling trade routes between East and West
- Han Chinese - Advanced bureaucratic civilization with the Mandate of Heaven
- Scythian - Nomadic horse warriors of the steppes
- Armenian - Mountain people controlling vital crossroads

### Ancient Religions
**Western Religions:**
- Roman Imperial Cult - State religion venerating the emperor and traditional gods
- Germanic Paganism - Warrior religion centered on courage and fate
- Celtic Druidism - Nature-based religion with priestly class
- Kushite Religion - African religion blending Egyptian and local traditions

**Eastern Religions:**
- Zoroastrianism - Persian dualistic religion emphasizing truth vs. falsehood
- Chinese Imperial Cult - Bureaucratic religion supporting imperial legitimacy
- Early Buddhism - Growing monastic tradition emphasizing enlightenment

### Technology System
The mod includes era-appropriate innovations that reflect ancient technological development:

**Tribal Innovations:**
- Iron Working - Advanced metallurgy for Germanic and Celtic tribes
- Warrior Lodges - Military brotherhoods for tribal societies
- Tribal Gathering - Democratic assemblies for decision-making

**Classical Innovations:**
- Aqueducts - Roman engineering marvels improving urban development
- Roman Law - Legal system bringing order and justice
- Greek Philosophy - Intellectual traditions advancing learning

**Eastern Innovations:**
- Silk Road - Trade networks connecting East and West
- Imperial Bureaucracy - Administrative systems for large empires
- Fire Temples - Zoroastrian religious architecture

### Historical Dynasties
- **Julio-Claudian** - The first Roman imperial dynasty
- **Arsacid** - Parthian royal house ruling Persia
- **Han** - Chinese imperial dynasty
- **Meroitic** - Kushite royal house of Nubia
- **Cherusci, Suebi** - Germanic tribal dynasties
- **Catuvellauni, Iceni** - British Celtic royal houses
- **Aedui, Arverni** - Gallic noble families
- **Artaxiad** - Armenian royal dynasty
- **Scythian Royal** - Steppe nomad dynasties

### Government Types
- **Imperial Government** - For major ancient empires (Roman, Chinese, Parthian)
- **Tribal Government** - For Germanic and Celtic tribal societies
- **Feudal Government** - For established kingdoms (Armenian, Kushite)

### Special Mechanics
- **Provincial Modifiers** reflecting historical characteristics:
  - Rome as the "Eternal City" with massive bonuses
  - Athens as a "Center of Learning"
  - Trade crossroads along the Silk Road
  - Germanic forests providing defensive bonuses
- **Character Traits** appropriate to the era and culture
- **Historical Events** potential for the ancient period

## Historical Accuracy

The mod prioritizes historical accuracy for the year 1 AD:
- **Roman Empire** under Augustus at its territorial and cultural zenith
- **Germanic Tribes** maintaining independence beyond the Rhine
- **Parthian Empire** as Rome's great eastern rival
- **Han Dynasty** ruling a unified China
- **Kingdom of Kush** as an independent African power
- **Celtic Druids** still practicing in unconquered Britain

## Installation

1. Extract the mod to your Crusader Kings 3 mod directory
2. Enable the mod in the game launcher
3. Start a new game and select the "Dawn of the Common Era" bookmark
4. Choose your ruler and begin your ancient empire!

## Compatibility

This mod is designed for Crusader Kings 3 version 1.12.* and should be compatible with most other mods that don't extensively modify the same systems. Some potential conflicts may occur with:
- Total conversion mods
- Other historical mods covering the same period
- Mods that extensively modify cultures or religions
