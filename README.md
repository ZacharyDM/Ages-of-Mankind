# Ages of Mankind - Crusader Kings 3 Total Conversion Mod

A comprehensive total conversion mod that takes players on humanity's complete journey from the Paleolithic era (2.6 million BCE) all the way to the Age of Revolution (1800 CE).

## 🎯 Vision

Experience the entire scope of human history in a single campaign. Start as a small band of hunter-gatherers struggling for survival in the Stone Age, and guide your people through technological innovation, cultural evolution, and societal development to become a sophisticated civilization ready for the Age of Exploration.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 🏛️ Complete Historical Progression
- **10 Distinct Eras**: From Paleolithic to Age of Revolution
- **Dynamic Government Evolution**: Start with bands, evolve through chiefdoms, kingdoms, and beyond
- **Authentic Technology Trees**: Research historically accurate innovations
- **Cultural Divergence**: Watch ethnic groups split and adapt to their environments

### ⚔️ Band Government System
- **Landless Nomads**: Begin as truly nomadic hunter-gatherer bands
- **Survival Mechanics**: Focus on finding food, shelter, and resources
- **Leadership by Merit**: Rule through prowess, wisdom, and innovation
- **Constant Movement**: No permanent settlements in early eras

### 🔬 Progressive Technology
- **Stone Tools**: Master the first human technologies
- **Fire Making**: Control the power that transformed humanity
- **Language Development**: Unlock complex communication
- **Agricultural Revolution**: Transition to settled life
- **Metallurgy**: Enter the Bronze and Iron Ages

### 🌍 Environmental Adaptation
- **Geographic Specialization**: Mountains, coasts, and deserts shape cultures
- **Climate Response**: Adapt to ice ages, warming periods, and seasonal changes
- **Resource Management**: Hunt, gather, and eventually farm for survival
- **Migration Patterns**: Follow game, resources, and favorable climates

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Installation**: Place mod files in your CK3 mod directory
2. **Launch**: Enable "Ages of Mankind" in the game launcher
3. **New Game**: Select the Paleolithic start date (2.6M BCE)
4. **Choose Your Band**: Pick from early African, European, or Asian cultures
5. **Survive and Thrive**: Guide your people through the challenges of prehistory
